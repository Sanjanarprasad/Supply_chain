## Critical Observations
* **Provenance Verification:** Confirmed high demand for transparent farm-of-origin data in organic and export segments.
* **Quality Discrepancies:** Identified recurring issues where physical product quality did not match supplier claims.
* **Interface Preferences:** Noted strong interest in QR-integrated solutions, provided the UI remains streamlined.
* **Accessibility Barriers:** Small-scale proprietors highlighted the need for an intuitive or simplified user experience.
* **Regulatory Compliance:** Export-level users prioritize robust certification records and digital audit trails.

## Strategic Conclusion
The data validates a market necessity for FarmTrace, provided the system focuses on operational simplicity for diverse user demographics.
